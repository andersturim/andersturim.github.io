About Anders

I was born in Seattle, Washington but was raised primarily in Tucson, Arizona. The most notable attraction of our city is our lack of winter and a line in a Beatles song, which you've very likely heard of. I moved to Montreal, Quebec to attend McGill University, and enrolled in the Bachelors of Arts program.

I currently am pursuing my degree in Geography with a concentration in Urban Systems. In addition, I am also pursuing two minor degrees in Computer Science and Environmental Studies.

Through my experiences at McGill, I've learned how to research effectively and quickly - the study of Geography is one of the few disciplines that is half an art and half a science and requires one to use many search engines and databases, all while keeping a tight due date in mind. As well, I've become an effective event organizer through assisting the planning and hosting process of several large McGill Underggraduate Geography Society (MUGS) events.
